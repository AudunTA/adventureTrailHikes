Our guides

At Adventure Trails Hikes, we employ local guides who know and understand the local terrain, but also have insight into the culture and history of the area. We believe in working with local communities to offer once-in-a-lifetime adventures that benefit everyone. Our guides are passionate about hiking and exploring the world by taking time to appreciate nature's small wonders and magnificent beauty. They will point out areas of interest, and will answer questions about flora and fauna found along the trail.

Sustainability

Sustainable tourism is important to us. Our world has amazing natural resources, and it is important that future generations can enjoy them as well. Not only do we want to ensure we don’t damage natural resources and local communities, but we want to work together to help them flourish.
